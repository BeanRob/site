grandma's chocolate fudge cake

ingredients

  • 4oz butter
  • 7oz sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 6oz plain flour
  • 2oz cocoa powder
  • 1 - 2 tsp vanilla extract

method

  1. Melt the butter in a pan. While it melts, beat the eggs.
  2. Add the butter and sugar to the eggs. Then, add the flour, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract, and beat well.
  3. Pour into a shallow tin which has been greased with butter and lined with greaseproof paper. Bake at 150Â°C for 30 minutes. If you prefer the cake to be less gooey and more solid, instead bake for 35-40 minutes.
