grimoire of characters

This is a collection of characters I have played in various games of Dungeons & Dragons. They have all been very fun to play, which is why I wish to chronicle them. Additionally, there will be some characters I am yet to play: I have many ideas, so it is useful to have somewhere to log them.

Tabletop RPGs are really a collaborative effort, so if you had a hand in creating any of the fun times I had with these characters, I thank you deeply. <3
